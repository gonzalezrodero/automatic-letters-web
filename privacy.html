Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

I am Daniel González, and I provide automated WhatsApp chatbot infrastructure for organizations. This Privacy Policy explains how I handle data when you interact with a chatbot powered by my technical services.

1. Data We Collect

When you interact with a bot running on my infrastructure, the system may temporarily process and store:

  • Phone Number: To identify the conversation and route messages correctly.
  • Chat History: To provide context for the AI to answer your questions accurately.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the chatbot service for the specific organization you are contacting. I do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. My system uses isolated databases (multi-tenant architecture) to ensure your data is only accessible to the organization you intend to communicate with.

3. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, modify, and completely erase your personal data from my servers.

You can automatically exercise your right to erasure (Right to be Forgotten) at any time through the WhatsApp chat interface. Please visit my Data Deletion Instructions page for the exact steps.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or how my technical infrastructure handles data, please contact the organization operating the bot directly, or contact me at gonzalez.rodero+privacy@gmail.com.
